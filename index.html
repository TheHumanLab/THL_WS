Nine structural layers. 68 questions and nine open response fields. Completed in writing at your own pace. The open responses are the most diagnostically significant component — they surface not just what you think, but how your system uses language and where it is trying to go.

STEP / 02 The Decode

Every word is read in full by a human practitioner. The structural pattern beneath your responses is decoded — not categorised, not processed, not summarised. Read. The decode requires up to ten hours of practitioner time per diagnostic.

STEP / 03 The Report

A Structural Decode Report is written and delivered within ten working days. Several thousand words. Second person throughout. It names your cognitive architecture, your decision structure, your stuck point — precisely and without softening — and the specific shape of your intelligence as it actually exists.
